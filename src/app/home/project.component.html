Introduction

This site is an aggregation of data from various systems that contain people and office data. It will help you find and connect with people or offices in the agency. Data on this site is cached every few days from the source systems (see date at the top of the page). The main sources of data are DOORs , Active Directory, Outlook Address Book, Human Resources (HRODS), Electronic Personnel Enrollment Credential System (EPECS), Investment Management Tool (IMT) , as well as some new mapping files to clean up the data.

If you come across bad data, let's clean it up! Just click the "Report Bad Data" link at the top.
